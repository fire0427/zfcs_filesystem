Audio Capture and Playback

This example demonstrates audio capture and playback ability of this EVM. The Multimedia framework used is GStreamer

NOTE: Please make sure a listening device is connected to the audio line out and a microphone is connected to the audio line in. If your EVM does not have an audio in, empty buffers will be recorded.

Multimedia Pipelines

Capture

gst-launch
 |_ alsasrc
   |_ wavenc
     |_ filesink

Playback

gst-launch
 |_ filesrc
   |_ wavparse
     |_ alsasink
